About us

The Silvassa Originals is a homegrown brand from the heart of Silvassa, dedicated to crafting personalized treasures with love and artistry. From elegant wedding and engagement hoops to custom t-shirts, phone covers, handkerchiefs, hairpins, and more — each piece is a blend of authentic hand embroidery and modern creativity. We turn your ideas into meaningful, made-to-order designs that celebrate individuality, tradition, and originality in every stitch.

Join us in reimagining handmade — beautifully, boldly, and originally.
